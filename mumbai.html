Processing Community Day - Mumbai

Jan 24th 2019 | Venue - Vile Parle

Processing community day India has been created by and for the local communities in India. The first ever processing community will be celebrated on 24th January 2019. The aim to celebrate #PCDMumbai is to bring the creative community together. It also gives an opportunity for enthusiast to get started and introduced to the cool things other people are doing in the area.

Date: 24th January 2019

Time: 3pm - 6pm

Place: NMIMS School of Design
